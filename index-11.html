ASA Summer Membership Drive

The American Sociological Association (ASA), a nonprofit professional association, held a summer membership drive to encourage sociologists to join and current members to renew their membership. I designed the banner used for the landing page and email blasts. I also designed social media posts for BlueSky, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and X.
